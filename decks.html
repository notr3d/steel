Steel decks

Zamil Steel produces its own shop drawings for the fabrication of your steel joists, trusses, structural steel components, steel deck and girts.

Our production equipment is at the leading edge of technology. We have continued to invest significantly in the best available equipment in the industry, including computer-aided manufacturing and numerically-controlled machinery. Zamil Steel has automated roll formers for steel decks.

Zamil Steel’s advantage has always been our ability to deliver on time, regardless of where or when you need our products. We know you cannot afford to keep your on-site erection crews waiting. We satisfy the requirements of your construction schedules.
